Paneer Veg Sauted

Breakfast




Chilli Paneer is a popular restaurant style Indo Chinese starter that is great for a dinner party with friends and family.A spicy, tangy paneer recipe that you can serve as is or along with a bowl of rice.





INGREDIENTS -
Paneer/Cottage Cheese
Tomato
Onion,Garlic and Chilly paste
bell peppers and chooped carrot

PROCEDURE -
1.Pour some oil into the pan and heat it warm. Then pour your chilli,onion and garlic ginger paste.Saute them until they turned to golden brown.
2.Add your chopped vegetables and saute them too.
3.then add soy sauce,vinegar,chilly sauce and tomato ketchup and mix it well until the color changes.
4.Add cornflour powder and water into a bowl, stir them well and add them to your pan. It will thicken the liquid.
5. Cook them for 10 minutes and add coriander and basil and serve them hot.
